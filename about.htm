I work in the Programming Language Systems Group at Microsoft Research.

My research interests and projects are in programming languages, compilers, and programming environments. Some of my work centers on lcc, a retargetable, production compiler for ANSI C. lcc is joint work with Chris Fraser. lcc is a component of the Zephyr project, which is part of the National Compiler Infrastructure.
